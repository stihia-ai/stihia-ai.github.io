# Design Context

## Users

- Primary viewers are security leaders and technical buyers evaluating AI security platforms.
- They are scanning a landing page to quickly understand product coverage, credibility, and enterprise readiness.
- The interface should help them grasp the value fast without visual clutter or consumer-app styling.

## Brand Personality

- Precise, credible, premium.
- The emotional goal is calm confidence rather than urgency or hype.

## Aesthetic Direction

- Dark, technical, restrained visual language with a refined enterprise feel.
- Small explanatory visuals should read like polished product schematics, not loud infographics.
- Avoid hard geometric heaviness, excessive text inside visuals, and attention-seeking accents.

## Design Principles

1. Prioritize clarity at thumbnail size.
2. Use restrained contrast and clean hierarchy instead of loud emphasis.
3. Prefer elegant flows, soft panel separation, and hairline connectors.
4. Keep text inside visuals minimal and deliberate.
5. Signal enterprise trust and sophistication over spectacle.
