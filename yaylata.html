Visit Yaylata!

Located on the rugged northern Black Sea coast, just 2 km south of the village of Kamen Bryag, the Yaylata National Archaeological Reserve is a stunning open-air museum where ancient history meets breathtaking nature. The reserve covers a seaside terrace separated from the sea by dramatic 50â€“60 meter high cliffs, offering panoramic views of the water.

Visitors can explore a fascinating "cave city" comprising 101 ancient dwellings dating back to the 5th millennium BC, alongside Thracian necropolises and the restored towers of an early Byzantine fortress from the 6th century AD. Beyond its history, Yaylata is famous for its natural beauty; it lies on the Via Pontica bird migration route and is blanketed with wild red peonies every spring.
